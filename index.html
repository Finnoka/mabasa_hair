Our Story

Where Korean artistry
meets your hair.

Mabasa was born from a deep passion for the refined, detail-oriented approach of Korean hair culture. Our team of expertly trained stylists brings the precision and craft of K-beauty to Brisbane — delivering results that genuinely transform.

Every visit begins with a thorough consultation. We listen, we advise, and we work with your hair's natural character to bring out its absolute best.

Before You Book

Booking &
Cancellation
Policy

We reserve each appointment time exclusively for you. Please review our policies to ensure a smooth experience for everyone.

Required Deposit
$30
Non-refundable · Required at time of booking
Deposit Requirement

To secure your appointment, a $30 non-refundable deposit is required at the time of booking. This applies to all services across both locations.

Cancellations & Rescheduling

Please cancel or reschedule at least 48 hours in advance. Cancellations, schedule changes, and no-shows leave gaps in our schedule that cannot always be filled with short notice.

No-Show Policy

Clients who do not attend their scheduled appointment without prior notice will forfeit their deposit. We appreciate your respect for our stylists' time and dedication.

How to Book

Contact us via Instagram DM @mabasa_hair, phone (Toowong: 0494 183 740 · Southbank: 0493 074 090), or email mabasa.hair@gmail.com. Please include your name, mobile, date, time, and service when enquiring.
