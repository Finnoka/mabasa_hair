Important Information

Booking &
Cancellation Policy

We reserve appointment times exclusively for you. Please review our policies before booking.

Deposit Requirement
To secure your appointment with Mabasa Hair Salon, we require a $30 non-refundable deposit at the time of booking. This applies to all services.
Cancellations & Rescheduling
Appointment times are reserved exclusively for you. Cancellations, schedule changes, and no-shows leave gaps that are difficult to fill. Please cancel or reschedule at least 48 hours in advance. The deposit is non-refundable under any circumstances.
No-Show Policy
Clients who do not attend their scheduled appointment without prior notice will forfeit their deposit. We appreciate your understanding and respect for our stylists' time.
How to Book
Reach us via Instagram DM @mabasa_hair, mobile (Toowong: 0494 183 740 · Southbank: 0493 074 090), or email at mabasa.hair@gmail.com. Please provide your name, mobile, preferred date, time, and service required.
